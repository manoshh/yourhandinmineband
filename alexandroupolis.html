Municipal Theatre of Alexandroupolis

03 March 2012

YHIM take "The Garden Novels" for a ride to the east, after being invited by Alexandroupolis' Cinema Club (K.L.A.). The concert was held at the Municipal Theatre of Alexandroupolis, maybe one of the last "classic" cinema halls in northern Greece! Special thanks to Mihalis, George, the Kafetzi family, all people from KLA, for making this happen !
